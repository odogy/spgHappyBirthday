Dear SPG...
You've brought us a great deal of joy through your music. But your impact on our lives goes so much further. Over this past year, we have formed lifelong friendships with people we met because of you. These friendships have changed our lives. We wanted to find some way to show you just how significant this is. So we invited members of this community to name 10 friends they believe they would never have met if StringPlayerGamer was not a part of this world. As you might guess, limiting ourselves to only 10 was really hard! Our lives are rich with friendships that you helped lay the foundation for. Please click Start to see a tiny fraction of the friendships that exist because of you.
